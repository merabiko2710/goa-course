zhiguli car

a car from the iron hearted Soviet Union. a gem of the 20th century USSR, this car still remains in the former countries of USSR. one of the first "modern style cars " in the world.manufactured in Russia and the Soviet Union by AvtoVAZ, during 1970-2012 and somewhat longer in some places abroad. For the majority of export markets however the cars were sold under the Lada brand.
